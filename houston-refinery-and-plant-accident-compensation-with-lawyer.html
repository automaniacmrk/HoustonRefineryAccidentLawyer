Houston Refinery and Plant Accident Compensation with Lawyer

The damages for cases that involve explosions, fires or other incidents at refineries and plants are usually very large due to the high number of victims and extent of the injuries. And for good reason: The worst oil and gas disasters have resulted in hundreds of serious injuries and multiple deaths.

The amount of compensation recoverable in your claim will depend on your individual losses. Generally, you may be able to recover damages for:

  • Health expenses
  • Lost wages
  • If you're unable or unwilling to work, your earning potential is lost.
  • Out-of-pocket expenses related to your injury (i.e., medications; a cane, crutches, or wheelchair; travel to and from doctor appointments; modifications to your home and/or vehicle; etc.)
  • Pain and suffering
  • Disability
  • Scarring and disfigurement
  • Loss of consortium

At Patrick Daniel Law, we are strategic in our approach to each case. We meticulously build your claim based on the evidence and the injuries you have suffered. We then relentlessly pursue all the responsible parties for the damages you are entitled to.

Wrongful Death Claims In Plant And Refinery Accidents

Fatalities are a common, tragic occurrence in oil and gas facility disasters. If you lose a loved one in a fire, explosion, or other accident at a plant or a refinery, our lawyers can pursue a wrongful death claim on your family's behalf.

Your family's losses will determine the damages that can be recovered in a wrongful-death claim. Generally, you may be able to recover compensation for:

  • Lost income
  • Inheritance and benefits lost
  • Cost of funeral services and burial or cremation
  • Household services and care taken away
  • Medical expenses prior to your loved one's death
  • Loss of consortium, care, and companionship
  • If the decedent had children, loss of parental guidance

Losing a close friend or loved one can be devastating emotionally. This can cause financial hardship for your loved ones. If your loved one worked in oil and gas and his or her death is the result of negligence on the part of the company or another party, you deserve to be compensated.
